IndabaX Sudan 2025 Attendees

The IndabaX is a locally-organized machine learning event, that helps spread knowledge and build capacity in machine learning. It is held in countries across our African continent. The IndabaX is a means with which to experiment with the ways in which we strengthen African machine learning and its local communities, allowing more people to contribute to the conversation around machine learning and artificial intelligence.

Important Notes:


The conference will be held from 10th to 14th December 2025.

Location: Online (on Gatherly Platform)

The timing and schedule will be sent to the selected people via email.
